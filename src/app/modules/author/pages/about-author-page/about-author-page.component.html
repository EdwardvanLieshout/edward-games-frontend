Edward van Lieshout is a 25 year old front-end developer from the Netherlands. His experience lies mostly in the Angular framework, in which he can effortlessly build reactive, responsive and component-based websites from scratch. Edward loves solving puzzles. Thus, he is most often found working on the js/ts side of the application. However, he can also write clean stylesheets and semantic HTML, with the help of scss and HTML5.

Although Edward's background is more on the technical side, he can also bring his own UX insights to the table, creating the much needed feedback loop between designers and developers. Edward has put his skills in practice for various customers, and for some of his personal projects. This portfolio website was made to show off some projects in which a multitude of web-development techniques were used.

In his spare time, Edward likes to play piano and produce electronic music. Ages ago, when he was a young lad, he hoped he could become the next aspiring Martin Garrix, but alas! Now he spends his days writing web-apps (no regrets).

Besides being a creative geek, Edward also enjoys playing video games with friends. Maybe one day Edward will make his own online game so he can finally win some.
